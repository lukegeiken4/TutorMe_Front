TutorMe for Students

TutorMe is an application used by volunteer tutors and students to match for outside tutoring in order to help YearUp students.

Tutoring can be requested for either English or Math subject areas and can help you get the extra help students need outside of the YearUp courses. You can also use TutorMe to accelerate and move ahead in your classes if you so choose. TutorMe is optional and free; it is for you to add a more personal touch to your learning and to help you in the specific areas that you need the most. Choose the best method of instruction for you, face-to-face or skype (availability for face-to-face may vary based on region). Select your availability on a weekly basis so that we can find tutors who can work with your schedule.
Fill in the personality/learning style assessment! Tutorme uses an intelligent algorithm to optimally match you up with a tutor who follows your learning style and personality as closely as possible.
